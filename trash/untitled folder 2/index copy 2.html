FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from an ad agency?
An agency leaves — advertising stops. You're left with only contract debt. Direct is a sales department that stays with you: team on your payroll, configured CRM, playbook, creatives. We build in 8 weeks — then it works for you.
What happens to our current sales team?
We resolve in discovery — three scenarios: Integrate (strong team, we update playbook and tools), Strengthen (there are gaps, we hire missing roles), or Partially Replace (if objectively needed, you decide).
If after 3 months results are weak, can we exit?
6 months is the minimum to reach target KPI. At 3 months of operation (month 5 from start) — checkpoint: On target → we continue, Close → we correct model, Doesn't work → we part ways with no penalties.
What if AI ruins a $200K–$2M deal?
AI only does work where it cannot make decisions affecting the sale. Mia answers typical questions, qualifies, books meetings. Does not name off-list prices, does not promise individual terms. Human escalation is automatic in 4 cases: off-list price, discount, low confidence score, or keywords like "lawyer", "contract", "escrow", "cancellation".
How much does it cost?
Structure: Retainer in two modes (months 1-3 build, months 4+ operations) + fixed percentage of closed revenue. Exact amount and percentage are calculated in discovery after reviewing project economics: GDV, sales cycle, unit types, market maturity.
What do we keep after the contract?
Team (SDR, managers) — on your payroll, they stay. CRM, playbook, knowledge base, creatives, landings — yours. AI agents — our IP. After contract they work by subscription $3.5k/mo or one-time purchase $50k. You can opt out — department works without AI, just slower.

Does your project fit Direct?

45 minutes. You show us the numbers — we tell you what to change, what KPI we can set, and if it makes sense to keep talking.
